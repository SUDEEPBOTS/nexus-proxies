# Nexus Proxies

## Register
product

## Platform
web

## Users & Purpose
**Users:** Individuals and developers who need to generate and format premium proxies from Webshare.io for Telegram.
**Purpose:** Provide a seamless, secure, and 1-click utility to convert raw Webshare API proxy lists into Telegram-compatible SOCKS5 connection links without exposing the API key to third-party servers.

## Brand Personality
- Fast, secure, and modern
- Glassmorphic UI with dynamic transitions
- Clean, crisp, and professional (Dark/Light mode adaptability)

## Positioning
The easiest and most beautiful way to format Webshare proxies into Telegram links instantly inside your browser.

## Anti-References
- Clunky, old-school form-based utility websites
- Bland, unstyled "developer only" tools
- Sites that look untrustworthy or bloated with ads

## Accessibility Needs
- High contrast for text readability on glassmorphic backgrounds.
- Clear visual feedback for actions (e.g., "Copied" states).
